# Project Description — Beto

## Overview

Beto is a voice-powered mobile copilot designed to help older adults use smartphones safely and confidently. The app simplifies everyday interactions with technology while actively protecting users from digital scams and fraud attempts.

Beto listens, understands, and assists through a simple conversational interface in natural Argentine Spanish. At the same time, it monitors on-screen context to detect suspicious patterns such as urgent money requests, fake family impersonation scams, phishing links, verification code requests, and remote-control app installation attempts.

When a potential scam is detected, Beto calmly warns the user using a clear and friendly voice interface, explains why the situation may be dangerous, and offers immediate actions like contacting a trusted family member with a single tap.

The platform is designed specifically for seniors, with large buttons, accessible interactions, voice-first UX, and minimal complexity.

## Problem

Older adults are increasingly targeted by sophisticated digital scams through messaging apps, phone calls, and social engineering attacks. These scams exploit urgency, fear, and emotional trust, often causing financial and emotional damage before victims realize what happened.

Most existing cybersecurity tools are too technical, reactive, or inaccessible for elderly users.

## Solution

Beto acts as a real-time safety companion for smartphone usage by:

- Assisting seniors with everyday smartphone tasks using voice.
- Detecting scam patterns directly on-device.
- Warning users before harmful actions occur.
- Connecting users with trusted family members instantly.
- Providing a calm, human-centered experience instead of technical security jargon.

## Key Features

- Voice assistant optimized for seniors.
- Real-time scam detection.
- Detection of suspicious language and phishing patterns.
- Large accessible interface.
- One-tap emergency family contact.
- Offline-first risk detection engine.
- WhatsApp and phone scam monitoring support.

## Technology

Beto uses an Android Accessibility Service to understand visible screen context. A local scam detection engine analyzes suspicious patterns offline for privacy and speed. Optional AI models help explain risks naturally to the user but do not make autonomous decisions.

## Impact

Beto helps older adults regain confidence and independence while reducing the risk of digital fraud. It also provides peace of mind for families concerned about online scams targeting elderly relatives.

## Vision

Our goal is to transform smartphones into safer and more accessible spaces for older adults through intelligent assistance, protection, and human-centered design.
